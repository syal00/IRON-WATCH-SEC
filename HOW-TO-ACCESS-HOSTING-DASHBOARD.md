# How to Access GoDaddy Hosting Dashboard

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Log In to GoDaddy

1. **Go to GoDaddy website:**
   - Visit: [https://www.godaddy.com](https://www.godaddy.com)

2. **Sign in:**
   - Click **"Sign In"** button (top right corner)
   - Enter your GoDaddy username/email and password
   - Click **"Sign In"**

---

### Step 2: Find Your Hosting

1. **Go to My Products:**
   - After logging in, look at the top right of the page
   - Click **"My Products"** (or it might say your name/account)
   - A dropdown menu will appear

2. **Alternative: Direct Link:**
   - You can also go directly to: [https://sso.godaddy.com/?realm=idp&path=/products](https://sso.godaddy.com/?realm=idp&path=/products)

---

### Step 3: Access Hosting Dashboard

1. **Find Web Hosting Section:**
   - In "My Products" page, scroll down
   - Look for **"Web Hosting"** section
   - You'll see your hosting plan listed

2. **Click Manage:**
   - Next to your hosting plan, click the **"Manage"** button
   - This opens your Hosting Dashboard

---

### Step 4: What You'll See

**Hosting Dashboard shows:**
- Your domain name (or temporary domain like `fnz.ca8.mytemp.website`)
- **"cPanel Admin"** button (top right) - Click this to access File Manager
- Website management options
- Settings and tools

## Alternative: Direct Access Methods

### Method 1: Direct cPanel Access
1. Go to: `https://your-domain.com/cpanel`
   - Replace `your-domain.com` with your actual domain
   - Or use your temporary domain: `fnz.ca8.mytemp.website/cpanel`

2. Enter your cPanel username and password
   - Username: Usually shown in Hosting Dashboard
   - Password: Your hosting password

### Method 2: Direct Hosting Link
1. Go to: [https://hostingmanager.secureserver.net/](https://hostingmanager.secureserver.net/)
2. Log in with your GoDaddy credentials
3. Select your hosting account

---

## If You Can't Find It

### Check These Places:

1. **My Products Page:**
   - Make sure you're on the "My Products" page
   - Look for tabs: "Domains", "Web Hosting", "Email", etc.
   - Click on "Web Hosting" tab

2. **Account Menu:**
   - Click your name/account icon (top right)
   - Look for "My Products" or "Manage Products"
   - Select "Web Hosting"

3. **Search:**
   - Use the search bar at top
   - Type "hosting" or "cpanel"
   - Look for hosting-related links

---

## What to Do Once You're In

### In Hosting Dashboard:
1. **Find "cPanel Admin" button** (usually top right)
2. **Click it** - Opens cPanel in new tab
3. **In cPanel, find "File Manager"** (under Files section)
4. **Click File Manager** - Opens file browser
5. **Navigate to `public_html`** folder

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see Web Hosting"
- **Problem:** Hosting not purchased or not active
- **Solution:** 
  - Check if you actually purchased hosting
  - Contact GoDaddy support
  - Check your email for hosting confirmation

### "Manage button is grayed out"
- **Problem:** Hosting might be pending setup
- **Solution:**
  - Wait a few hours for setup to complete
  - Check your email for setup confirmation
  - Contact GoDaddy support

### "I forgot my password"
- **Solution:**
  - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
  - Follow password reset steps
  - Or contact GoDaddy support

### "I can't log in"
- **Solution:**
  - Make sure you're using the correct GoDaddy account
  - Try password reset
  - Contact GoDaddy support: [https://www.godaddy.com/help](https://www.godaddy.com/help)
